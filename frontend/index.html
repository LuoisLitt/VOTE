How It Works

1. Hold Tokens

You need tokens from the configured token contract to participate. Your voting power equals your token balance.

2. Connect Wallet

Connect your Dusk wallet to prove your token holdings and see active proposals.

3. Cast Your Vote

Vote Yes or No on proposals. Your vote weight is determined by your token balance (1 token = 1 vote).

4. Results

After voting closes, results are tallied. The option with the most weighted votes wins.
